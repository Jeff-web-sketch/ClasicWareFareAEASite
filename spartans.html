Civ Bonuses

Greek Olympians

Certain Champs and Special Infantry 3% fater walk speed, Melee units +5% damage, Ranged units +3% damage, 5% more accurate, and +1 range.

Hellenic Architecture

Civic Centers, Temples, Special Structures, and Wounders +10% health and +20% capture points.

Agoge

Champion Hoplites are available in phase 1 with -10% repeat time, as Young Hoplites (Paidiskoi) they cost -50 food and metal and take only 16s to train. However, they do 25% less damage, have -2 hack/pierce resistance and -25 health. They premote to normal Spartiates (Hebontes) which are aviallble to train in Town Phase and can eventually promote to Olympic Spartiates (Hippeis).

Melee infantry +5% more damage.
