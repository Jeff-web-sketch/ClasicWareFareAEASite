Spartans

Civ Bonuses

Champions

Champion Hoplites are available in phase 1, and may promote to Hippeis. They attack 10% faster than other champion spearman. Phase 1 champions take 20% less train but
have 50 less health, -1 of hack and peirce resistance and do 15% less dmg. It is gained back through phasing up

Melee Infantry

Melee Infantry 5% more dmg and gains an additional 5% p2 but Sparta does not have Will to Fight.

Perioikoi Class

Melee and Ranged 10% less dmg, Ranged, 10% less accurate. However, they gather resources 5% faster, have 5% faster walk speed, cost 10 less wood and train 10% faster

Helots

50 health, -20% dmg, 15% less accurate -5 range and +250 repeate time but train 15% faster and gather resources 25% faster and don't count torwards population.

Women

Women 5% faster gathering/build rates rate, and 2% faster movement speed. Also 50% melee dmg and 0.5 of each resistance.

Warrior Culture

-10% maximum population limit.

Team Bonuses

Heroes train for free; Melee Infantry Heroes 15% more Pierce/Hack/Crush dm Infantry +1 hack/pierce resistance and promote 10% faster.

Heroes

Available p2, but 1/2 health and 20% less dmg. Gains the health and dmg when you go p3 Champion Infantry +15% melee attack, +1.5 hack/pierce, +5 Crush resistances
and +25 health. Spearman 5% walk and 10% run speed. Available on p3 version only

Agis

Own and allied soldiers -40% metal cost and -25% training time. Agis 33% health and 5 of each resistance

Brasidas

Javelineers +2 crush, hack, pierce resistance, +3 capture attack. Range 60 Helots promote 50% faster to neodamodes
